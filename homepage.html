FAIRYTAIL 100 YEARS QUEST
              (NEW SEASON)

The 100 Years Quest arc, also known as Fairy Tail: 100 Years Quest (フェアリーテイル １００年クエスト Fearī Teiru Hyaku-nen Kuesuto), is the story arc that covers the entirety of the Fairy Tail: 100 Years Quest series,chronologically taking place after the Fairy Tail series.
DR. STONE (SEASON 3)

Dr. Stone is an isekai in disguise. The adaptation of one of Shonen Jump’s most popular manga, which is now two episodes into its first season, has the premise of a sci-fi adventure, but it’s got the same exposition problems as any other alternate-world anime.
BUNGO STAY DOGS (SEASON 4)

Bungo Stray Dogs is primarily a supernatural show that focuses on a group of young detectives who possess supernatural powers. The main protagonist of the show is Atsushi Nakajima.
CYBERPUNK: EDGERUNNERS
             (NEW SERIES)

A Street Kid trying to survive in a technology and body modification-obsessed city of the future. Having everything to lose, he chooses to stay alive by becoming an Edgerunner a Mercenary outlaw also known as a Cyberpunk.
KAKEGURUI TWIN
                (NEW SPIN-OFF)

Kakegurui Twin (賭ケグルイ双〈ツイン〉, Kakegurui Tsuin) is a spin-off title that started serialization in Gangan Joker on September 19, 2015. The series is written by Homura Kawamoto and illustrated by Kei Saiki. The story focuses on Mary Saotome, one year before Yumeko Jabami transferred to Hyakkaou Private Academy.
